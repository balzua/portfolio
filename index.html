Game Deck

Demo / Github

This is a MERN stack application that allows a user to track their video game collection. Users can add games by searching a third-party API, then rate them from 1-5 stars and assign them to categories such as 'Wishlist' or 'Currently Playing'. They can also sort their collection, filter by video game platform, and see their taste in genres at a glance.
